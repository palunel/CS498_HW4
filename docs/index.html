How long does an MCS-DS take at UIUC?

by

Paul Nel

CS498 Data Visualisation - 2019

As part of my journey through the MCS-DS program I kept detailed logs of the time I spent on each subject and each task. I would like to present some of the detail here as part of the Week 7 project assignment.

To date I have spent about 730 hours on this program. 330 days have elapsed since my first course started in Fall 2018 of which I spent at total 216 actually studying. On these days, I spend on average 3 hours and 36 minutes every day. This averages out at about 15 hours and 45 minutes each week including all breaks!

The first figure shows how this time has been split amongst the 6 subjects I have taken to date Hover over the slices to see how much time I spent on each and click through on each slice for more detail!
